Confederate Monuments Explorer
Confederate monuments are polarizing relics from the past century that celebrate soldiers, military officers, or political figures from the Confederacy and the Civil War era. For many Americans, especially those in northern or western states, they may not know the extent or prevalence of these monuments. Many southerners may assume that these monuments are relatively common. This map hopes to facilitate exploration of the spatial distribution of confederate monuments in the United States.
